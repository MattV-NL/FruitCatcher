Welcome to my version of a Fruit Catcher Game!! Have fun!

    How to play
  • Left arrow moves basket Left
  • Right arrow moves basket Right
  • You can use the arrow keys on your keyboard or use your mouse or
    touchscreen on the buttons below
  • Catching a fruit earns the player points
  • Look out for the bombs! They cause the player to lose a life
  • Missing a fruit will make the player lose a life as well
